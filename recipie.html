Steak Fries

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Combine oil, paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Place potato wedges in the bowl and coat with spices. Transfer potato wedges to a large ungreased baking sheet (do not overcrowd). Bake until the potato wedges are golden and crisp (about 40 minutes). Serve immediately!
