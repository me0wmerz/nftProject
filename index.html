PAIR UP



The Virtual CookBook For Whatever's In Your Fridge!



Do you ever have a hodgepodge of ingredients in your fridge that you don't know what to do with? We sure do! And when your stomach is growling, it's even more important to figure out what's for dinner! We've created a tool to make meal planning quick and easy! Simply select which ingredients you've got, and you'll get tailor-made recipes and a corresponding YouTube video to show you how to follow along. Better yet, our app will even provide precise nutritional information and recommend the perfect beer pairings of your finished dish!
